1 Registration

Create your MTGO-DB account to get started.

1
Register for an account

Create your MTGO-DB account with a valid email address.


2
Confirm your email

Check your inbox and click the confirmation link.

2 Import from GameLogs

Upload your raw GameLogs and DraftLogs to MTGO-DB for processing.

1
Download the Zip Script

Download and run the Zip-MTGO-Logs script. A .zip file containing your GameLog and DraftLog files will be generated.


2
Navigate to Import

Click on Processing â†’ Import GameLogs in the navigation menu.


3
Upload Your Files

Navigate to the .zip file created in Step 1 and upload it.


4
Process Data

Click Import. Your files will be processed in the background and a Load Report will be emailed to you upon completion.

3 Clean Your Data

Clean and process your data, ensuring it is accurate and complete.

1
Best Guess Deck Names

Apply the best guess deck names to the Match.P1_Subarchetype and Match.P2_Subarchetype columns.


2
Get Missing Game Winners

Sometimes, a game winner can not be determined. In this case, you can manually set the winner after reviewing the game.


3
Apply Associated Draft IDs

Associate a Match with a specific Draft.


4
Revise Match Data

You can revise the Match data (P1/P2_Archetype, P1/P2_Subarchetype, Format, Limited Format, Match Type) after the initial import.

4 Next Steps

1
Browse Data

Search and filter your Matches, Drafts, and Play-by-Play data.


2
View Analytics

Explore your match history and performance statistics using prebuilt dashboards and reports.


3
Export Data

Download your data to Excel.
