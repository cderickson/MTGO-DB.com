Mox Data

Mox Data is a cloud-based data ingestion tool and data analytics platform for Magic: The Gathering Online.

Players can upload and process raw gameplay files created during online play. The processed data is then loaded and stored in our database, after which users can clean and enhance their own records. Data is presented in tables and dashboards that help players track results, evaluate performance, and analyze play patterns.

Head to the Getting Started page for step-by-step instructions showing you how to load, clean, and view your data.
